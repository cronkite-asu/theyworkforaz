TheyWorkForAZ was developed in the New Media Innovation Lab at Arizona State University based on the ideas of Mark Ng and phxdata. It was inspired by mySociety's theyworkforyou. Nathan Anderson designed the site, with help from Heather Billings and ???

TheyWorkForAZ draws on bills and other records made available online by the Arizona Legislature. It is not, however, an official record of legislative proceedings.

Our Philosophy



Democracy is the lifeblood of our nation. For democracy to thrive, however, voters must be active and informed. TheyWorkForAZ uses publicly available information in new ways to help you track issues and give your lawmakers feedback on their performance.

Are your representatives truly working for you? TheyWorkForAZ helps you find out.

Open-Source
